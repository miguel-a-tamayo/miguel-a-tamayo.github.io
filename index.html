ABOUT ME

Space… It offers so many possibilities just like life! If space has taught me anything, it is that just like a rocket fighting against gravity with a mission to explore and accomplish new milestones, we should also chase and reach for our dreams despite the forces trying to bring us down. And so that’s what I did. Being born in Colombia, I didn't have much so I learned to value the small things in life and moving to the United States opened my eyes to a world of opportunities.

When I was introduced to space in high school by watching a SpaceX video, I sat on my chair thinking how amazing it is that humans come together to create something so complex and innovative. I love space because we leave borders, race, cultures, beliefs, and everything else that sets us apart behind and come together as one, humans, to explore and understand the world we live in.

With my growing interest for space and the aerospace industry, I have decided to study Computer Engineering with a concentration in robotics and Control as well as embark on my own projects to hopefully one day be part of the teams that design the next generation of spacecraft to explore beyond out planet.
